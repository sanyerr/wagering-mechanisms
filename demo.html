Brier Betting Mechanism

Interactive demo based on Lambert et al. (2015)

How it works: Players each stake money and report their probability estimate for an event. After the outcome is revealed, the total pot is redistributed: more accurate forecasters gain money at the expense of less accurate ones. The mechanism is budget-balanced (no money enters or leaves), sybilproof, and incentive-compatible (honest reporting maximises expected payoff).

Formula Reference

Payoff for player i:
πi = (wi / W) + (wi / W) · ∑j≠i (wj / W) · [ (pj − x)² − (pi − x)² ]

wi = player i's wager  |  W = total wagers  |  pi = player i's probability estimate  |  x = outcome (0 or 1)

The payoff πi is the fraction of the total pot returned to player i. Multiply by W to get dollars. Profit = payout − wager.
